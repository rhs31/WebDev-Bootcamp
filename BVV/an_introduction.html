An Introduction

Blue Vista Villa sits atop a hill on St. Croix's north shore, offering breathtaking 270° views of the Caribbean Sea and Salt River National Park, with clear evening views of the distant lights of St. Thomas.

Blue Vista Villa accommodates up to 8 guests in 4 air-conditioned bedrooms, each with private bath. Enjoy the heated pool, outdoor spa with hydro-massage, generous pool-deck, air-conditioned exercise / recreation room, wi-fi as well as a fully equipped office area. Children are welcome.

Why rent a villa? It's a private vacation getaway, just for you, your family and friends, where you can feel at home. You'll have full use of all of the amenities and not be bothered by any other guests. You'll have much more space than at any hotel, and you can socialize throughout the villa, instead of being restricted to your rooms. Finally, the villa costs less than a comparable hotel.
